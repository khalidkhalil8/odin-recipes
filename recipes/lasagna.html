Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a comforting Italian classic made by layering tender sheets of pasta with a rich, savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and a generous amount of melted mozzarella. The sauce, often made with ground beef or sausage, is simmered with onions, garlic, and tomatoes for deep flavor, while the ricotta is mixed with egg and herbs for extra creaminess. Each layer is built carefully, creating a beautiful cross-section of pasta, sauce, and cheese that bakes together into a bubbling, golden masterpiece. Perfect for family dinners or gatherings, lasagna is as impressive to serve as it is satisfying to eat.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pund ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 ounces marinara sauce
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 half cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons dried Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 half teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh basil or parsely

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, sauté the diced onion and minced garlic until fragrant and translucent.
  4. Add the ground beef (or sausage) to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in the marinara sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  8. Layer 4 cooked noodles over the sauce, then spread a third of the ricotta mixture on top.
  9. Sprinkle a third of the shredded mozzarella cheese over the ricotta.
  10. Repeat layering two more times, finishing with mozzarella and remaining Parmesan on top.
  11. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake an additional 25 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  12. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before serving to set.
